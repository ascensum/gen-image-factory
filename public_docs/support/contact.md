# Contact Us

Need help or have questions about Gen Image Factory? We're here to assist you.

## Email Support

For support inquiries, feature requests, or general questions, please contact us at:

**Email:** [admin@shiftlinetools.com](mailto:admin@shiftlinetools.com)

## What to Include

When contacting support, please include:
- A clear description of your question or issue
- Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable)
- Your operating system and application version
- Any relevant screenshots or error messages

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days.
